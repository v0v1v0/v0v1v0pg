insurgencies

Description

A subset of insurgencies data in Iraq (March to June 2007)

Usage

insurgencies

Format

A tibble with 68573 rows and 4 variables:

date

Date (YYYY-MM-DD)

longitude

Longitudes (decimal)

latitude

Latitudees (decimal)

type

Types of insurgencies (improvised explosive devices (IED), small arms fire (SAF), or other)
