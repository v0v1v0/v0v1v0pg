Testing Goodness-of-fit with the Kernel Density Estimator

Description

Tests of goodness-of-fit based on kernel smoothing of the data.

Details

Package: GoFKernel
Depends: R (>=2.17.3), stats, KernSmooth (>=2.23-8)
