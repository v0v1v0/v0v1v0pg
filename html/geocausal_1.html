airstrikes R Documentation

airstrikes

Description

A subset of airstrikes data in Iraq (March to June 2007)

Usage

airstrikes

Format

A tibble with 3938 rows and 4 variables:

date

Date (YYYY-MM-DD)

longitude

Longitudes (decimal)

latitude

Latitudees (decimal)

type

Types of airstrikes (airstrikes or shows of force (SOF))

airstrikes
