Dot Product

Description

Computes the dot product of two vectors.

Usage

dot(a, b)

Arguments

a,b

Two vectors of equal length.

Details

Vectors can be of any length provided they are equal.

Value

A vector of length 1.

Author(s)

Adrian Baddeley and Tom Lawrence

See Also

cross

Examples

  dot(c(2,1),c(-3,4))
