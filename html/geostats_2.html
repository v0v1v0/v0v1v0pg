ACNK R Documentation

A-CN-K compositions

Description

Synthetic A (Al_2O_3) – CN (CaO+Na_2O) – K (K_2O) data table.

Examples

data(ACNK,package='geostats')
ternary(ACNK,type='p',labels=c(expression('Al'[2]*'O'[3]),
                               expression('CaO+Na'[2]*'O'),
                               expression('K'[2]*'O')))
