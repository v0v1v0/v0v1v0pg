List of Minerals

Description

International Mineralogical Association (IMA) Commission on New Minerals, Nomenclature and Classification (CNMNC) approved list of minerals, names and abbreviations (Warr 2021).
Retrieved from RRUFF (Lafuente et al. 2015).

Usage

mins

Format

A data frame with 5763 minerals and their names, symbols (abbreviations) and chemistry.
Symbol, Name, Chemistry
